Our Story

Welcome to Timothy Ware Photography! My passion for photography began when I was gifted my first camera at the age of 10. Over the years, I honed my skills and developed a unique style that combines traditional techniques with modern aesthetics.

My goal is to capture the genuine emotions and beautiful moments of your special day. Every wedding is unique, and I strive to tell your love story through stunning imagery that you will cherish for a lifetime.
